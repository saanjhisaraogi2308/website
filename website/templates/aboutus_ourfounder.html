Meet Our Founder - CA Manish Saraogi

CA Manish Saraogi, Founder of Manish Saraogi & Associates, established the firm in April 2001 in Siliguri, West Bengal, with the vision to provide reliable, insightful, and client-focused financial and advisory services. With over 24 years of professional experience (2001–2025), he continues to lead the firm with dedication, precision, and a strong ethical foundation.

Born on 30th June 1973, he completed his schooling at Mahbert High School in 1990, followed by higher secondary education from Mount Hermon School in 1992. He pursued Commerce at Siliguri College of Commerce, graduating in 1994 with a Bachelor of Commerce (B.Com) degree. He then qualified as a Chartered Accountant and became a member of the Institute of Chartered Accountants of India (ICAI).

CA Manish Saraogi is known for his hands-on approach and in-depth understanding of taxation, audit, and business advisory services. His leadership and commitment to professional excellence continue to guide the firm in delivering value-driven solutions to clients across sectors.

Introducing Our Future Partner - Krish Saraogi

Krish Saraogi, the next-generation professional set to join the leadership team, brings youthful energy, a modern perspective, and strong academic credentials to the firm. Born on 5th July 2005, he completed his schooling from Don Bosco High School and pursued his undergraduate studies at St. Xavier’s College, Kolkata.

Krish has successfully cleared both CA Intermediate Group 1 and Group 2 examinations and is currently pursuing his articleship training as part of his Chartered Accountancy qualification. With only the CA Final examination left, he is on track to become a full-fledged Chartered Accountant.

His involvement ensures continuity, innovation, and an unwavering commitment to the firm’s values and excellence in client service.
